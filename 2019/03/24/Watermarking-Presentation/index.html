The Effect of Whitening on Error Rates

From Wikipedia

Definiton

A whitening transformation or sphering transformation is a linear transformation that transforms a vector of random variables with a known covariance matrix into a set of new variables whose covariance is the identity matrix, meaning that they are uncorrelated and each have variance 1. The transformation is called “whitening” because it changes the input vector into a white noise vector.

What is white noise vector

A random vector (that is, a partially indeterminate process that produces vectors of real numbers) is said to be a white noise vector or white random vector if its components each have a probability distribution with zero mean and finite variance, and are statistically independent: that is, their joint probability distribution must be the product of the distributions of the individual components.

White noise

所有频率具有相同能量密度的随机噪声

Calculation

$$
Define\ X\ to\ be\ a\ random\ vector\ with\ convariance\ matrix\ {\Sigma}\ and\ mean\ 0
\
The\ matrix\ {\Sigma}\ can\ be\ written\ as\ the\ outer\ product\ of\ X\ and\ X^T\
\
{\Sigma}=E[XX^T]
\
Define\ {\Sigma}^{1/2}\ as:
\
{\Sigma}^{1/2}({\Sigma}^{1/2})^T={\Sigma}
\
Define\ the\ new\ random\ vector\ Y={\Sigma}^{-1/2}X.\ The\ convariance\ of\ Y\ is
\
Cov(Y) = E[YY^T]\=E[({\Sigma}^{-1/2}X)({\Sigma}^{-1/2}X)^T]\=E[({\Sigma}^{-1/2}X)(X^T{\Sigma}^{-1/2})]\={\Sigma}^{-1/2}E[XX^T]{\Sigma}^{-1/2}\={\Sigma}^{-1/2}{\Sigma}{\Sigma}^{-1/2}\=I
\Thus,\ Y\ is\ a\ white\ random\ vector.
$$

Chapter 7 Analyzing Errors

就算是再精妙的水印系统也不可避免地存在错误。本章我们讨论三种类型的错误：假阳性错误、假阴性错误与消息错误。假阳性错误是检测器错误地判断水印存在，相对地，假阴性错误是检测器错误地判断水印不存在。消息错误指的是水印检测器错误地解码了一段消息（水印检测器的翻译错误）。

水印系统在设计阶段必须考虑其错误率是可接受的，因此，对可能发生错误的建模变得十分必要。建立错误模型的目的有两个，其一是模型帮助我们选取检测阈值以满足水印系统的要求，其二是为了控制特定问题的错误率不会超过太多。

我们通常会假设水印上增加的干扰性噪声是白噪声（也就是所有频率具有相同能量密度/概率密度的随机噪声），但是也有很多情况下并不都是白噪声。在这种情况下先前的错误模型（Error Model）会变得非常不可靠。因此在7.5中我们讨论如何运用白化滤波器让噪声去相关。
